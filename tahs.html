Myrna Rosen

TAHS 1955 Class Reunion

class reunion invite

Myrna created the invitations to her high school reunion of the class of 1955. The piece uses a fun green background as negative space to define the modern roman capital letters of the school, TAHS, in combination with an uncial hand. The inside of the invite describes various activities that white reunion offers, using playful attention to details such as the dash between the time stamp on activities.
