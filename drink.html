This piece is one of Myrna's favorite works. Created with a brush on toothy paper, she describes the continuous effort to mix just the right moisture of ink and water to achieve this look. The words are penned in a capital italic hand. This has since been used as a greeting card, and exists as one of Myrna’s portfolio pieces.
