bar mitzvah invite

An Thank you letter to those that attended the the bar mitzvah of Myrna’s niece. Below pictures the full hand scripted invitation to the party, made in English Roundhand with the pointed nib and purple gouache. Myrna still has a package of unused invitations and thank you letters.
