Myrna Rosen

Kiss Me Kate

commissioned work

A man commissioned Myrna to write a one of shakespeare’s sonnet’s, Kiss Me Kate, that he was to present to a woman as a marriage proposal. The body of text is written in secretary, the hand Myrna Rosen is most well known for. The first letter of the piece, ‘M’, is a Blackletter capital guided with metal.

Hand: Secretary
