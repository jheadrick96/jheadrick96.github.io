gift

A gift to her husband Jack on there 22nd anniversary. Myrna remembers that there was a blizzard that kept her from going out to buy him a present, so she made this piece as a gift. This is one of two nearly identical pieces in different colors- both roman capitals A-Z written with the round B nib. It now hangs framed in the dining rom. This piece was also one of many that Myrna has entered into an exhibit.
