Myrna Rosen

ABCs

exercise

An personal exercise of English Roundhand capitals, made while Myrna Rosen was still an apprentice to Arnold Bank. It is composed of the alphabet on dark blue paper in white quash, with a pointed pen.

Hand: English Roundhand
