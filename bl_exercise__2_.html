Myrna Rosen

Blackletter Exercise

Exercise

This piece is one of Myrna’s favorites. On her beach vacation with her family, she describes sitting up to the lunch bar at the beach out with her three colors and playing. This piece is an exercise i noted modern blackletter with uncial capitals, written on black paper. Myrna fondly remembers this as such a relaxing time.

Hand: modern blackletter
