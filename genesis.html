Myrna Rosen

Genesis

biblical passage

A passage of scripture from Genesis, the first chapter, verses 1-5, describing God’s creation of the heavens and the earth. Penned in a cursive italic script, this passage was submitted to show and later used as a greeting cards. The visual breaks in the passage come in the word “GOD”, where the italic capitals are emphasized.

Hand: Cursive Italic
