Myrna Rosen

Play

exercise

While on a business trip with her husband, Myrna took the top drawer out of the hotel desk and used it as an easel. With just a travel set of water colors, Myrna created this piece. Myrna often reminds her students that they have to play, and claims she only ever gets to d o that when she "leaves her house".
