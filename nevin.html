Myrna Rosen

J. Nevin Garber

commissioned eulogy

Commissioned by the Madison international Corporation, Myrna penned an eulogy for one of the deceased board members, J. Nevin Garber. This elegant piece utilizes the combination of the English Roundhand in ink to reflect the sentiment of the company, and the Roman capitals in graphite to resect J Nevin Garber. this piece reflects one of the many official honors, Certificates, and Awards that Myrna has been commissioned to write.
